❓ FAQ

How secure is my data?

We use industry-standard AES-256 in GCM mode, which provides both encryption and authenticity. Keys are never stored in plaintext and are protected using RSA-2048.

What is the "Cipher Image"?

It is the encrypted binary version of a medical image. It looks like random data to anyone without the correct RSA private key, making it safe to send over public networks.

Can I reset my password?

Yes, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. A reset link will be sent to your registered healthcare email address.
